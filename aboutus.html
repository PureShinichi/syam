ABOUT US

HISTORY OF ALIBABA STATION

Alibaba Café, founded in 2021 by a family, takes its name from P. Ramlee's iconic 1961 film
Alibaba Bujang Lapok.The film humorously adapts Ali Baba and the Forty Thieves, following
Alibaba, a kind but poor man who discovers a robbers' treasure. With the help of the clever Marjina,
he outwits the thieves, while his greedy brother Kassim Baba
faces consequences.

About Company's

Vision

Great service, quality coffee, unique menus, and events drive café success.

Mission

Opening up more job opportunities for the surrounding population. A growing café creates jobs for baristas, cooks, and cleaners while supporting local suppliers.

Target Audience

Cozy cafés with Wi-Fi attract professionals, students, and families. Healthy options draw health-conscious customers, while unique specialties appeal to tourists.
